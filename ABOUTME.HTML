My Role Models

Prophet Muhammad Saw
"Do not grieve. Indeed, Allah is with us (At-Tawbah 9:40)." – Prophet Muhammad Saw
I deeply love and admire Prophet Muhammad (SAW) because of his unparalleled kindness, wisdom, and humility. He was a true leader who always prioritized compassion, justice, and mercy, showing us the importance of treating others with respect and empathy. His patience in the face of adversity, his dedication to spreading peace, and his unshakeable trust in Allah continue to inspire me every day. Prophet Muhammad (SAW) taught us that the greatest strength lies in gentleness and the power of forgiveness. He is not just a role model, but a guiding light for all of humanity, and I strive to live by his teachings and reflect his character in my own life
My Grandparents
"Hold steadfast to your prayers, and let your heart shine through acts of charity." – Abu Bakar and wife
I love my grandparents so much. Even though they are at an age where many would expect them to stay at home and rest, they continue to work hard to earn their own living, never depending fully on their children. Despite this, they never hesitate to give to others through charity (sedekah). To me, they are everything — a true inspiration of strength, generosity, and selflessness
GOT7
"As a group, we are not just a team, we are a family. We work together, support each other, and grow together." – GOT7
I love Got7 because they never fail to impress me. Their dedication to their music, their continuous growth, and their unwavering bond as a team are truly inspiring. The way they support and motivate each other as a family is something I deeply admire. Their teamwork and friendship are exceptional, and they remind me that when you have a strong, supportive group of people by your side, anything is possible. Got7's ability to stay united and positive, no matter the challenges they face, is something I look up to every day.
